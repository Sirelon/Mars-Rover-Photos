## 📝 Full "What's New" (expanded — website / GitHub release)

**Mars Rover Photos 4.0 — buckle up, this is a big one. 🚀**

### 🔥 The stuff you'll notice first

🛰️ **The lost rovers are back from the dead.** Spirit, Opportunity, and Curiosity
went completely dark — you literally couldn't browse them. We dragged all three
back online, hundreds of photos each. *This is the headline.*

❤️ **Double-tap to like.** Smash a photo, watch the heart pop, feel the buzz. Like
it once and it's liked *everywhere*, instantly — no refresh, no waiting.

✨ **Photos finally move like they should.** Tap one and it glides into fullscreen.
Swipe down to fling it away. And no more black loading flash — images snap in
instantly and sharpen to full-res the moment you zoom.

### 👍 Genuinely handy

📅 **Get where you're going, fast.** Jump straight to any sol or page, and tap a
camera to instantly filter the feed to just those shots.

🧭 **Picks up where you left off** — close a photo and you land right back where you
were scrolling.

### ✨ Nice to have

🎨 **A glow-up for the whole app** — redesigned Rovers and About screens, plus
dark/light theme switching smooth enough to flip just for fun.

📱 **Better on big screens** — tablets get roomier, smarter layouts.

…and a pile of polish: cleaner dates, sharper icons, a slick splash screen, and a
brand-new app icon.

**Mars has never looked this good. Go explore. 👽**
